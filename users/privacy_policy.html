Privacy Policy

Who are we?

PrimerStock is a new web application that provides a personalized and dynamic primer database for private laboratory use, allowing a quick and effective primer search.

Laboratory and user registration process

In order for you to register on our application, we need some of your personal data:

First Name
Last Name
Email
Country
Lab data

Why do we collect your personal and lab data?

In order for us to have a registration of current users, as well as providing you with the history of modification of the primers, we need to have your personal data.

Where is this information being kept?

This information is being kept on the website’s servers, which are located in Spain, EU. The servers are secured as best as they can be under all web standards.
This information is also being transmitted to our email address which is hosted on Google’s servers inside and outside EU. Google’s services are fully in compliance with GDPR and US-EU Privacy Shield.

Safety of your personal and lab data

All information transfer on our website and to our email address is made via secure encrypted channels in order to keep your personal data safe.

Who has access to your personal data?

PrimerStock for maintainance purposes. Our IT team has access to our registration systems, but only for maintenance purposes. The IT team does not observe, collect or use your personal data.

For how long is this information being kept?

PrimerSTOCK will delete your personal data as well as any lab data 30 days after the signing off process has been completed.

Sending your personal data to third parties

PrimerStock does not send any of your data to third parties

What automated decision making and/or profiling we do with user data

PrimerSTOCK does not use automated decision making and/or profiling with user's data.

What data breach procedures we have in place?

All your personal data is being kept in a secure environment. If data breach happens, we will inform local authorities about the data breach and our hosting service. We will also inform you about the data breach.

How to withdraw consent to use of personal data?

If you want to withdraw a consent, please send us an email at: primerstock.customer@gmail.com

Right to erasure

Your personal data will be deleted 30 days after the signing off process has finished. However, if you want this data to be deleted before, please contact us at: primerstock.customer@gmail.com

Review your data

Request the information about what personal data we keep, request deletion or rectification by sending an email to: primerstock.customer@gmail.com
